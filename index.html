Basic

Sowing the Seeds of Enterpreneurship

The Basic track is for colleges which don't have an active Entrepreneurship Cell functioning. Teams which are enthusiastic to spread awareness about entrepreneurship and form an Entrepreneurship Cell from scratch in their college/institution are invited to participate in this track. Minimum 3 team members are required for participation.

Structure

Rules

Registered teams to adhere to the following rules throughout the competition.
More rules can be added at the discretion of E-Cell, IIT Bombay.

  • Only one team will be allowed to represent a college in the challenge
  • In case of multiple entries from the same college, the teams would be clubbed together in a single team.
  • For selecting a Team Leader from a college, we will connect the entries before 23rd September. After mutual agreement between multiple teams within 10 days, Team Leader should be decided and an official document with new team members should be submitted.
  • If multiple entries come after 23rd Sept., the first entry reserves the right to select the team leader and further entries would be clubbed.
  • *New Rule* While organizing any session which asks you for photos, please include one photo showing the NEC flex. In cases of the sessions organized on/before 24th September (This Sunday), you might be asked for a valid date-proof of the event. Please note that this is just a one-time investment. You can use the same flex in all the subsequent events.
  • Please find attached NEC Brochure here. This can be used to show to your faculty when required.
  • You can add other team members after registration but it is preferable that you do it as soon as possible. However, you must have atleast 3 members in the team prior to registration.
  • Winners of last year are not allowed to participate in the same year this time.
  • Please Ping us on Whatsapp for any doubts rather than calling us .

Guidelines

Team Size: Minimum - 3 Members; Recommended - 15 to 20 Members

  • Teams who are participating for the first time & don't have an E-Cell in their college are advised to register in the BASIC track.
  • Previous teams who were a part of the BASIC track but not in the TOP 15 are allowed to register in BASIC track only if there is a change of more than 50% in the team.
  • Teams who took part in ADVANCED track last year are not allowed to register in BASIC track.

NOTE : The Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Bombay reserves the final right to place a particular team in a fitting track.
